# From Dull to Doctor (Book and Free Blueprint)

## Content
"From Dull to Doctor" is a book by Dr. Matt Jones, described as the full, step-by-step blueprint of how he stripped his life and routines back and rebuilt them from the ground up — a more comprehensive version of the "subtraction" approach covered in the video, which he says only allows space for a limited amount of information. He states it took him three years to write, and that it is comprehensive across all aspects of life, covering how he "rewired his brain and body for success" and how a reader can do the same.

He frames the book's value through his own trajectory: going from tutoring seven days a week just to keep up with school and feeling behind many of his peers, to becoming a medical doctor with multiple master's degrees (one of which he completed simultaneously while in medical school), while also building a thriving social media career, maintaining relationships, and what he describes as a beautiful life overall. He says he wrote the book because he deeply wishes he'd had access to this kind of guide when he was struggling.

For people not ready for the full book, he also offers a free 30-day blueprint, linked in the video's description, which he frames as an effort to make this kind of guidance accessible to people at different starting points.
